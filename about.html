About Tic-Tac-Undertow

How to play!

  • Pick an adversary to play against and prepare to populate the ocean with your own fish species.
  • Choose a difficulty level.
  • Rock, paper, scissors to see who will go first.
  • Player 1 gets to be the Beta Fish. Player 2 gets to be the Puffer Fish.
  • Click in a square of your choice to enter your fish.
  • Now, it's the next player's turn. Keep taking turns until somebody wins.
  • If the match ends in a draw, press reset to start a new match.

How to win!

  • Overtake an entire row (down, across or diagonal) to win the game.
